Puneeth Rajkumar

Actor, Singer, and Producer


Early Life

Puneeth Rajkumar was born on 17th March 1975 in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu, India. He is the youngest son of legendary actor Rajkumar and actress Parvathamma Rajkumar.


Career

Puneeth Rajkumar made his acting debut as a child artist in the film Premada Kanike in 1976. He made his debut as a lead actor in the film Appu in 2002. Since then, he has acted in numerous successful films, including Abhi, Arasu, Jackie, Raajakumara, and Yuvarathnaa.


Awards and Honours

Puneeth Rajkumar has won several awards for his contributions to the Kannada film industry, including three Filmfare Awards and a National Film Award. In 2019, he was honored with the Padma Shri, the fourth highest civilian award in India.
